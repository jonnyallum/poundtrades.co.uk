These ASCII mockups give you an idea of how the app would look when running. The actual app would have a dark theme with yellow accents, images for listings, and a smooth, responsive interface.

To see the app running on your device:

1. Make sure you have Expo Go installed on your mobile device
2. Run `npx expo start` in the project directory
3. Scan the QR code with your device's camera (iOS) or the Expo Go app (Android)
4. The app will load on your device
